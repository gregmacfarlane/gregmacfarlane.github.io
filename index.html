Biography

I am an assistant professor in the Civil and Environmental Engineering department at Brigham Young University. I research transportation engineering and planning, with a specific emphasis in using modern passive data products to build models of travel behavior and land development.

Interests

  • Travel Demand Modeling
  • Land Use Modeling
  • Passive Transportation Data
