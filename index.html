I am an assistant professor of Civil Engineering at BYU. I am interested in transportation forecasting models, including activity-based models of travel behavior and applications of emerging datasets and spatial econometrics. I am particularly interested in improving the methods of modeling and how the tools of transport modeling might also be applied in other fields.

I teach courses on sustainability, transportation engineering, transport modeling, and traffic simulation.
